Become a Volunteer

We are always looking for volunteers who are fun, creative, patient, and passionate. Join us and you will be rewarded with personal growth and community.

What Makes a Good Volunteer

Working with young people is a privilege. We are looking for people who have a sense of humor, are respectful, patient, creative, collaborative, and passionate about youth work.

What You Get from Volunteering

Our Volunteers often tell us that they get far more back from giving a bit of their time and passion. Benefits include personal growth, impact, community, voice, new experiences, and recognition.

Things You Need to Know

Volunteers must agree to uphold our values, abide by our policies, hold a current role, undertake training, and provide personal information. Membership is by mutual agreement.

If you're interested in volunteering with us, please contact us for more information and to discuss opportunities.
